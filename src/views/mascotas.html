My Pets

Meet my beloved companions

Coockie

Coockie is an **Ancestral Female Cockatiel**. Females of this variety are characterized by their solid grey bodies, duller orange cheek patches compared to males, and fine yellow barring on the underside of their tail feathers.

Perla

Perla is a **Pearl Female Cockatiel**. This mutation is known for the beautiful scalloped "pearl" pattern on the wings and back. While males lose this pattern after their first molt, females retain these spots throughout their lives.

Sol, Mona & Ash

A wonderful trio: **Sol** is a cheerful Australian Budgie. **Mona** is a Lutino Female Cockatiel, distinguished by her yellow plumage and red eyes. **Ash** is a White-faced Male Cockatiel, a mutation that lacks the orange cheek patches entirely.

Galleta

I am grumpy only when you bother me. However, if you rub my head, I am very affectionate!

Snowball

I am a very relaxed cat. You can pet me anywhere, I don't mind. I won't bite even if you annoy me—I am a total angel.

Nacho

I am a little earthquake! I love scratching my toys. I get scolded when I try to escape or scratch the furniture. I am affectionate, but if you bother me, I won't bite; I'll just walk away from you.
